About The Registrar

The Registrar is the primary information network serving the stabilized vibrational states of The Loom. Initially developed by the Stellar Concord for official communications, it has expanded to include academic, commercial, and personal regsites.

Access Levels

Many regsites require specific access credentials. Concord personnel, registered Mage Houses, Scholasticae members, and approved civilians have varying levels of access to restricted materials.

Network Security

All Registrar traffic is monitored by Concord Arbiters for vibrational contamination. Attempting to access restricted materials without proper authorization may result in permanent network access revocation.
